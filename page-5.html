How I feel in tune with myself

As I observe myself throughout the young adult phase, I recognize there are so many big decisions in my life that I just followed my gut feelings. With all the plans and checklists that I create everyday, I see myself following logics and rules. However, I do appreciate my inner voice and would love to create more space for myself to practice and take care of my intuition. Because those are times I feel my most genuine thoughts and feelings.

Matcha making ritual

Making matcha drinks is one of my rituals to refresh and get ready for new beginnings. The taste of matcha gives me the sense of refreshment and new beginnings.

I sometimes have matcha with milk, both hot and cold. I also make myself matcha tea for an afternoon rewind. Matcha is also my go to replacement for coffee!

Journaling practice

Journaling helps me keep track of my thoughts and emotions. As a person who overthinks and has more than ten ideas going on all the time, I always make sure myself express those by writing down.

Besides those busy times, I keep practicing journaling even in my most neutral state. It helps me appreciate present moments and stay focused. I have three different journals: one to record my daily days, another to write down my thoughts in Vietnamese to fully express them, the last to write my very last thoughts before bed.

Inspired people

I find myself learning best when I am surrounded by people with similar free-spirited mindsets and hobbies.

I often share my thoughts and new ideas with friends when I feel my strong intuition to execute them. Besides, I follow some youtubers whose energy flow matches mine. I admire how they show up to the world, to their people, and to their life moments.
